Sherlock is a browser-based research workspace that combines AI-powered analysis with visual tools for investigation, monitoring, and structured reporting. Bring your own API key for any supported AI provider, and Sherlock keeps all your data locally in your browser — no server, no account required.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your API key">
    Go to **Settings → Runtime** and enter your API key for Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter. Your key stays in your browser and is never sent to Sherlock servers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a workspace">
    Open **Files** and click **New Workspace**. Give it a name and choose a domain scope that matches your research area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch an investigation">
    From any workspace, click **New Run** to open the run setup. Choose your scope, purpose, and model, then launch your first AI investigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Sherlock is a bring-your-own-key (BYOK) application. You need at least one API key from a supported AI provider to run investigations or use the chat feature. Browsing your existing workspaces and artifacts works without a key.
</Note>
